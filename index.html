Welcome, To Green Horizon

Welcome to our website dedicated to raising awareness about climate issues and their impact on our daily lives. Through this platform, we aim to provide clear and easy-to-understand information about climate change by asking simple questions that encourage you to think and engage with this critical topic. We believe that awareness is the first step towards a solution, so we offer content designed to simplify scientific concepts and highlight the challenges we face, while also presenting ideas and solutions that everyone can contribute to in order to protect our planet.
